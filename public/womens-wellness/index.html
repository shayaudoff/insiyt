02 — Birth Hypnosis

Calm, Confident, Present

Hypnobirthing uses the deep relationship between mind and body to transform the birth experience — replacing fear with trust, tension with ease, and uncertainty with calm preparedness.

What Is Birth Hypnosis?

Birth hypnosis — also known as Hypnobabies — is a comprehensive, evidence-based childbirth preparation programme that uses self-hypnosis, deep relaxation, and positive imagery to help women approach labour and birth with confidence and calm.

Shaindel has been a certified Hypnobabies Instructor for over three decades, guiding countless women through this practice as part of her wider commitment to empowering women through every life transition.

What It Offers
  • Deep relaxation techniques for labour and birth
  • Tools to release fear and reframe birth as a natural process
  • Partner involvement and support skills
  • Positive birth imagery and self-hypnosis practice
  • Preparation for all birth settings and outcomes
  • A grounded, calm nervous system from pregnancy through birth
"A calm mind and a relaxed body create the optimal conditions for birth — and for the beginning of new life."

03 — Perinatal Mental Health

Supported Through Every Threshold

Pregnancy and the postpartum period are among the most emotionally complex transitions a woman can move through. You deserve care that holds the full picture — your body, your mind, your story.

🌱

Prenatal Mental Health

Anxiety, depression, and emotional overwhelm during pregnancy are far more common than many women are told — and far more treatable. INSIYT offers integrated psychiatric and somatic support tailored to the unique needs of pregnancy.

  • Prenatal anxiety and depression
  • Birth-related fear and trauma
  • Relationship and identity shifts
  • Mind-body support through each trimester
🌸

Postpartum Support

The postpartum period is often described as a fourth trimester — a time of profound change and vulnerability. INSIYT's whole-person approach offers the emotional and psychiatric support that new mothers genuinely need.

  • Postpartum depression and anxiety
  • Birth trauma processing
  • Adjustment to motherhood
  • Body-based regulation and recovery
🤲

Shaindel's Approach

Drawing on over thirty years as a Certified Nurse Midwife and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, Shaindel holds a rare dual understanding of the physical and psychological landscape of perinatal care.

  • Integrated psychiatric evaluation
  • Somatic and body-based support
  • Medication management when indicated
  • Individual and group psychotherapy
