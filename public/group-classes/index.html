Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I wear or bring?
+
Wear comfortable, stretchy clothing you can move in freely. For yoga and dance classes bring a water bottle. For drumming circles everything is provided — just bring yourself. Mats and props are available for yoga sessions.
What is your cancellation policy?
+
Cancellations made at least 24 hours before your class receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable but can be applied as a credit toward a future class. No-shows are non-refundable.
Do I need any prior experience?
+
Not at all. All group classes are designed to be accessible regardless of background or experience. Our approach is about meeting you where you are — not pushing you toward a performance standard.
How do I book a private session with Shaindel?
+
Select "Private Somatic Session" or "Therapeutic Touch Session" in the booking flow above. You can also start with a free 20-minute discovery consultation to explore which approach is right for you before committing to a full session.
Is there parking?
+
Yes, free parking is available on site. The studio is located in Airmont, NY — exact address is sent in your booking confirmation email.
Can I gift a class to someone?
+
Gift certificates are available — please contact us directly and we'll set one up for you.
Is my payment information secure?
+
All payments are processed by Square — a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Your card details are never stored on INSIYT's servers. Square handles all tokenisation and charging securely.
