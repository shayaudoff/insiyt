Menopause Support

Menopause support at INSIYT addresses emotional, physical, and nervous-system changes during this major life transition.

Book Consultation Back to Home
01

Assessment

We map your current patterns, symptoms, and goals to build the right treatment sequence.

02

Regulation

We begin with practical nervous-system tools and interventions to reduce overwhelm.

03

Integration

As stability grows, we deepen emotional processing and long-term behavior change.

How This Helps

Care may include therapy, somatic regulation, and psychiatric support when helpful.

Sessions focus on helping you feel informed, stable, and connected to your body as it changes.

Included Focus Areas

  • Mood and anxiety support
  • Sleep and stress regulation
  • Personalized transition planning
