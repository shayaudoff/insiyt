A Career Built on Whole-Person Care

Psychiatric Care

Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner

Board Certified — Advanced Certificate, Hunter College Bellevue School of Nursing, NYC

Shaindel is a Board Certified PMHNP offering full-scope Psychiatric Mental Health services through INSIYT — treating a range of psychiatric conditions with an integrated, whole-person approach. INSIYT is dedicated to the treatment of conditions that have been shown to benefit from Ketamine administration as an adjunct to individual and group psychotherapy. Shaindel sculpts the ketamine experience uniquely for each patient to optimise its treatment effects.

Women's Health

Certified Nurse Midwife

CNM · Hypnobabies Instructor · Founder of SIYT & Healing Through Movement Groups

As a Certified Nurse Midwife, Shaindel has been educating and empowering women for the past three decades. Through her roles as Midwife, Doula, Hypnobabies Instructor, and Yoga Teacher, she discovered that the most impactful interventions on quality of life were consistently rooted in the care of emotional and mental health — wherever she met her patients on their journey.

Yoga & Movement

Registered Yoga Teacher — Trauma Informed

RYT-E · 200hr Training at FLOW Yoga, Bergen County · Yoga Alliance Certified

Subsequent to her RYT certification, Shaindel trained with renowned trauma researcher Bessel Van Der Kolk in the use of rhythm and movement in trauma healing. She has also attended an immersive retreat in Body Psychotherapy led by Karin Gurtner, developer of Slings Myofascial Training, and Joeri Calcius PhD. These experiences inform her offering of SIYT — Somatic Immersion Yoga Therapeutics.

Advanced Training

Transforming Touch & Ketamine Practitioner

TEB Certified · Ketamine Training Center — Full KAP Certification

Shaindel has completed training as a Transforming Touch Practitioner in TEB (Transforming the Experience-Based Brain) and is fully certified in Ketamine Assisted Psychotherapy through the Ketamine Training Center — bringing the most current evidence-based approaches to her practice at INSIYT.

Foundations

Inpatient Psychiatric Nursing & BSN in Psychology

Group Work Focus · Whole-Person Lens from the Start

Prior to her advancement in women's health, Shaindel received her BSN in psychology and worked as an inpatient psychiatric nurse with a focus on group work. Her commitment to view each person through the lens of their whole personhood — mind, body, and soul — has guided every chapter of her career.

"
The body, mind and emotions are interwoven and manifest illness in a multiplicity of ways that are interconnected and multifactorial. From this vantage point of the person's wholeness, profound multi-system healing is supported and possible.

— Shaindel Udoff, Founder of INSIYT

Her Vision

Truly Integrated, Multi-Layered Healing

Shaindel's vision in her treatment of individuals is truly integrated and multi-layered. Over the years of caring for and educating women, she discovered that the most impactful interventions — on both quality of life and physical health — were rooted in the care of the emotional and mental health needs of her patients.

This was true whether she met them as a Nurse Midwife, Doula, Hypnobabies Instructor, Yoga Teacher, or now, as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. She continues to be passionate about supporting and empowering women, promoting perinatal mental wellness, and walking with women through their life transitions.
